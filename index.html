Main Page


This is the website created to demonstrate basic website development skills for Task 1 of the TDT1 course at WGU. The navigation bar above will direct you throughout the site.

The purpose of this course is to educate independent authors on up-to-date methods for posting Amazon ads for their own works of fiction in a way that is light on wallets and calendars.



Ultimately this is just the very beginning of this course. We will start with Lesson One, which is analyzing sites like Amazon to find a "Comp List" for your work of fiction. For an example of such a search, click here: Amazon Comp Search.
